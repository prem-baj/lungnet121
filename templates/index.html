The LungNet121 app is trained on a dataset of 2,000 X-ray images and delivers a 97% accuracy rate in detecting COVID-19. Harnessing the power of advanced deep learning algorithms based on DenseNet121 neural network architecture, our app provides rapid and reliable diagnoses to assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions for their patients. Trust our app to deliver optimal care with confidence, knowing that it has been rigorously tested and trained to provide the highest level of accuracy.
